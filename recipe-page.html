Simple Pancakes

This simple pancake recipe is easy to make and perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Add the eggs and milk to the dry ingredients.
  3. Stir until the batter is smooth.
  4. Pour the batter onto a heated pan and cook until golden.
